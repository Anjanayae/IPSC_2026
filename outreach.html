IPSA Outreach Session at NSSS-2024, Goa

The Indian Planetary Science Association (IPSA) is proud to share the success of our recent outreach session held during the National Space Science Symposium (NSSS-2024) in Goa. This event was a significant step in our ongoing efforts to promote planetary science and inspire the next generation of scientists and researchers.

About the Outreach Session

The outreach session, held on the sidelines of NSSS-2024, attracted a diverse audience, including students, educators, researchers, and space enthusiasts. Our aim was to foster a deeper understanding of planetary science and to ignite curiosity about the universe among attendees.

IPSA Stall and Breakout Session

  • Awareness Stall: IPSA set up a stall to create awareness about our association, its mission, and activities. The stall attracted many visitors who were eager to learn more about our initiatives and how they could get involved.
  • Membership Certificate Distribution: During a special breakout session, we had the honor of distributing membership certificates to our esteemed life members. This session was a wonderful opportunity to recognize and celebrate the commitment of our members to the field of planetary science.

Impact and Feedback

The outreach session, stall, and breakout session were met with overwhelming enthusiasm and positive feedback. Participants expressed their appreciation for the opportunity to learn directly from experts and engage in meaningful discussions about the latest advancements in planetary science. The session not only enhanced their understanding of the subject but also inspired many young minds to pursue careers in space science.

Looking Ahead

IPSA remains committed to our mission of advancing planetary science and fostering a culture of scientific inquiry. We will continue to organize outreach sessions, educational programs, and collaborative events to engage and inspire communities across India and beyond.

Stay connected with us for more updates on our upcoming events and initiatives. For further information, please visit our website regularly and follow us on our social media channels.
