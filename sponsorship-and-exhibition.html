Message from the Organizers

The 7th Indian Planetary Science Conference (IPSC 2026) scheduled to be held during 23-25 March 2026, will be hosted by the Indian Institute of Technology Indore (IIT Indore), Madhya Pradesh, India. The conference aims to bring together researchers, faculty, students, and professionals across planetary science disciplines to present research results, foster collaborations, and discuss future directions in planetary and space science research.

IPSC 2026 is expected to be attended by around 300+ researchers and professionals from all over India and abroad. This event brings together scientists, engineers, policymakers, and other stakeholders from around the country to discuss the latest research and developments in planetary science and its applications. Organizing a conference of this scale is a massive undertaking and is only possible with the support of various organizations, including industry partners.

We are inviting industry partners, organizations, and relevant stakeholders to sponsor IPSC 2026. We offer several levels of sponsorship, ranging from Platinum to Bronze, each with a unique set of benefits and recognition. This presents a remarkable opportunity for organizations to exemplify their unwavering commitment to the field of planetary science, fostering innovation in space exploration, planetary research, climate studies, and related domains. Sponsors will have the opportunity to showcase their expertise, products, and services to a highly targeted audience of researchers, scientists, and industry professionals. In addition, sponsors will have the opportunity to share their ideas, network with other industry leaders, and develop new collaborations.

The support of industry and other organizations is vital in making this event successful and in helping advance the field of Planetary Science. We look forward to a positive response and whole-hearted cooperation in successfully conducting the conference.

Gold

Silver

Bronze
