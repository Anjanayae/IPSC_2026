About the Conference

IPSC 2026

We are thrilled to announce that the Seventh Indian Planetary Science Conference (IPSC-2026) will be held in 2026 at the prestigious Department for Astronomy, Astrophysics and Space Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Indore.

This highly anticipated event, organized by the Indian Planetary Science Association (IPSA), marks the seventh IIT Indore conference hosted by IPSA, continuing our tradition of fostering collaboration and innovation in planetary science.
