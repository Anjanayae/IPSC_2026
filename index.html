Welcome to IPSC 2026

The Seventh Indian Planetary Science Conference

will be organized along with S2:STEP2026 at IIT Indore

under the theme "Planetary Sciences and Exploration"

We are thrilled to announce that the Seventh Indian Planetary Science Conference (IPSC-2026) will be held in 2026 at the prestigious Centre for Space Science and Technology, Indian Institute of Technology Indore. This highly anticipated event, organized by the Indian Planetary Science Association (IPSA), marks the fourth IPSC conference hosted by IPSA, continuing our tradition of fostering collaboration and innovation in planetary science.

About IPSC 2026

IPSC 2026 will unite leading scientists, researchers, and enthusiasts from around the globe to discuss the latest discoveries, research, and advancements in planetary science. The conference will feature keynote, invited and contributary talks, panel discussions, and poster presentations, covering a wide range of topics related to planetary science and exploration.

As it is concurrent with Space for Sustainability: Science, Technology, Education and Policy, IPSA 2026 will further expand the scope of discussions. Topics will include recent advances, results, and studies related to the atmosphere, surface, and interior of planetary bodies in the solar system, planetary processes, and early solar system evolution.
