About the Conference

IPSC 2026

We are thrilled to announce that the Seventh Indian Planetary Science Conference (IPSC-2026) will be held in 2026 at the prestigious Centre for Space Science and Technology, Indian Institute of Technology Indore.

This highly anticipated event, organized by the Indian Planetary Science Association (IPSA), marks the fourth IPSC conference hosted by IPSA, continuing our tradition of fostering collaboration and innovation in planetary science.
